Frequently asked questions

How do I change the categories I receive emails for?

Go back to the sign-up page and enter your email address. Select the new categories you want, then click Start watching. Your preferences will be updated immediately — no need to unsubscribe and re-join.

How do I add WhatsApp delivery?

Go to the sign-up page, enter your email, select your categories, then toggle on Add WhatsApp messages. Enter your UK mobile number and submit. We'll start sending your nightly pick to WhatsApp as well as email.

How do I cancel WhatsApp delivery?

Go back to the sign-up page, enter your email and select your categories, but leave the WhatsApp toggle off. Click Start watching and your WhatsApp delivery will be cancelled. You'll continue to receive email picks as normal.

How do I unsubscribe from everything?

Scroll to the bottom of the sign-up page and click Unsubscribe. Enter your email address and confirm — you'll be removed from all future sends immediately.

How often will I receive emails?

We only send when there's something genuinely worth watching in your categories. On a quiet night, you won't hear from us. There's no fixed schedule — quality over quantity.

Why haven't I received anything yet?

A few things to check:

  1. Check your spam or junk folder and mark us as safe.
  2. It may simply be a quiet night — we don't send unless something's worth it.
  3. Make sure you completed sign-up and saw the "You're in." confirmation screen.

Do you share my email or phone number?

No. Your details are only ever used to send you What 2 Watch picks. We never share, sell, or pass them to third parties.
